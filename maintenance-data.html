Maintenance Data Management & Analytics System

Tontrac Plant and Engineering LTD • Accra, Ghana • 2025

Project Overview

In heavy industries such as mining and construction, maintenance operations often suffer from fragmented records, inconsistent data, and limited visibility into equipment performance. This project was designed to solve these challenges by building a centralized maintenance data management and analytics system that supports better decision-making and long-term equipment reliability.

Objectives

  • Consolidate scattered maintenance records, lubrication schedules, and work history into a single structured database
  • Develop interactive dashboards that provide real-time visibility of equipment performance and maintenance compliance
  • Reduce reliance on manual reporting and improve the accuracy of maintenance data
  • Create a strong data foundation for future predictive maintenance and condition-based monitoring

Approach & Implementation

The project began with a detailed assessment of existing maintenance workflows and data gaps. A normalized MySQL database was designed to capture equipment master data, maintenance work orders, lubrication activities, spare parts usage, and failure events. Interactive Power BI dashboards were developed to visualize key performance indicators such as equipment availability, maintenance compliance rates, backlog trends, and recurring failure patterns. Standardized data entry templates and automated validation rules were introduced to improve data quality at the source. The system was rolled out in phases, starting with critical equipment, and training was provided to field teams and supervisors to ensure proper adoption and data integrity.

Key Results

  • Created a single, reliable source of truth for maintenance data across operations
  • Enabled faster identification of problematic equipment and recurring issues through visual dashboards
  • Improved maintenance compliance tracking and accountability across teams
  • Significantly reduced time spent on manual report generation and improved reporting accuracy
  • Established a strong data foundation that can support future predictive maintenance and machine learning initiatives
